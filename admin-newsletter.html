How to Post a Newsletter

  1. 1 Fill in the Issue Date and Display Title below (e.g., "Week of May 19, 2026").
  2. 2 Click "Add Item" to add each announcement. Fill in the title, description, and pick a category color.
  3. 3 When finished, click "Generate & Download newsletter-data.js". A file will download automatically.
  4. 4 Upload the downloaded newsletter-data.js to the website folder, replacing the old file. The newsletter page will update immediately.
