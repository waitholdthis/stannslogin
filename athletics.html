Catholic School Athletics in Fayetteville

Faith, Teamwork & Sportsmanship

St. Ann Catholic School students in grades 5 through 8 compete as part of a joint athletic program with Saint Patrick Catholic School. Together, our students represent Catholic values on and off the field, building character through competition, discipline, and community.

St. Ann + St. Patrick Athletic Partnership

St. Ann Catholic School partners with Saint Patrick Catholic School (SPCS) to field competitive athletic teams for students in grades 5-8. This collaboration gives students the opportunity to compete at a higher level while strengthening the bond between two of Fayetteville's Catholic school communities. Learn more at spcsnc.org →

Grades 5-8 eligible to participate in all athletic programs
