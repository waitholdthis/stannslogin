Before & After School Care

St. Ann Catholic School's Before & After Care program provides a safe, structured, and nurturing environment for students before and after the regular school day. The program operates on all days when students are in school, giving families the flexibility they need while maintaining the values of our school community.

Before Care

7:00 – 8:00 AM

Drop off early and start the school day with confidence. Beforecare opens if school has a delayed opening.

After Care

3:00 – 6:00 PM

Safe, supervised care until 6:00 PM. Aftercare closes if school closes early due to weather or emergency.

What's Included

Homework Time
Indoor & Outdoor Activities
Healthy Snack

Homework time is non-negotiable — all students are expected to use the structured study period to complete assignments. The program applies the same discipline standards as the regular school day.

Hourly Rates

August Billing: All students attending Before/After Care during August are billed on an hourly basis. Hourly billing is also available as an alternative to the monthly rate year-round — contact the coordinator to switch.

Program Policies

Late Pickup Fee

A fee of $5.00 per student is charged for every 5-minute period (or portion thereof) after the 6:00 PM program closure. Please arrange timely pickup.

Billing Changes

Families wishing to change between monthly and hourly billing must provide two weeks' written notice to the program coordinator before the change takes effect.

No Refunds

All fees paid are non-refundable. This applies to both monthly and hourly payments regardless of attendance or school closures.

Weather & Closures

Aftercare closes whenever school closes early. Beforecare adjusts its opening time if the school announces a delayed opening. The program does not operate on non-school days.

Discipline Code

The school's full discipline code applies to Before & After Care. Students are expected to follow all school rules and treat staff and peers with respect at all times.

Emergency Contacts

Parents and guardians must maintain current emergency contact information at all times. Notify the coordinator immediately of any changes to pickup authorization or emergency contacts.
