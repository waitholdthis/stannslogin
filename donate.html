Give to St. Ann Catholic School

St. Ann Catholic School is a parochial elementary and middle school in Fayetteville, North Carolina. We are part of Saint Ann Catholic Parish and the Diocese of Raleigh. Your gift helps our school continue forming students who enter to learn and depart to serve. The official donation checkout opens securely through St. Ann's current BetterWorld-powered donate page.
