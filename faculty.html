Dedicated Educational Professionals

Our faculty consists of licensed professionals who maintain high expectations for all students while providing the individual attention and support each child needs to succeed.

Every member of our teaching staff is committed to educating the whole child, recognizing students' multiple intelligences and varied interests. We establish strong bonds with all students and their families in our safe, nurturing learning environment.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At St. Ann Catholic School, our faculty embraces a holistic approach to education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit of every student. We believe in:

  • Differentiated Instruction: Recognizing that each student learns differently and adapting our methods accordingly.
  • Character Formation: Developing moral and ethical reasoning alongside academic skills.
  • Community Partnership: Working closely with families to support each child's growth and development.
  • Continuous Growth: Engaging in ongoing professional development to stay current with best practices.
  • Faith Integration: Weaving Catholic values and teachings throughout the curriculum and school experience.
