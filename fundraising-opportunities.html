Upcoming Opportunities

RaiseRight Everyday Fundraising

Ongoing Gift cards, online shopping, dining, and travel

RaiseRight lets families support school fundraising through routine purchases. Participating brands share a percentage of qualifying purchases back to the fundraising organization, with earning options available through gift cards, online shopping, local dining, and travel bookings.

How families earn Shop with participating brands and a portion of eligible spending supports the fundraiser.
Why it is easy No door-to-door selling, event planning, minimums, or seasonal campaign window.
Ways to participate Use gift cards, shop online, dine locally, book travel, or use the RaiseRight app.
Need school setup info? Contact the school office for St. Ann participation details before creating or joining a program.
Visit RaiseRight Browse Brands Estimate Earnings

Double Good Popcorn Fundraising

Campaign-based App-powered Pop-Up Store fundraiser

Double Good helps groups raise money by selling premium popcorn through personalized Pop-Up Stores. Sellers share their store links by text, email, or social media, and supporters order online from anywhere in the U.S.

How it works Download the app, set up a fundraiser, invite sellers, and launch shareable Pop-Up Stores.
Group earnings Groups keep 50% of every bag sold, with no setup fees, handling fees, or minimums.
Easy fulfillment Double Good pops, packs, and ships orders directly to supporters, so families do not handle delivery.
Campaign energy Short selling windows, app tracking, and leaderboards help keep sellers engaged.
