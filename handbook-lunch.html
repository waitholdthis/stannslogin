Online Lunch Ordering

St. Ann Catholic School uses My Hot Lunchbox for convenient online meal ordering. Parents can pre-order hot lunches for their students directly through the My Hot Lunchbox platform — no more cash in backpacks.

My Hot Lunchbox

Order hot lunches online at your convenience. Choose from available menu options, manage your student's account, and pay securely — all in one place.

How to Get Started

  1. 1
    Create an Account — Visit the My Hot Lunchbox website and register as a new parent. You will need your student's name and school information.
  2. 2
    Find St. Ann Catholic School — Search for St. Ann Catholic School, Fayetteville, NC when setting up your account.
  3. 3
    Add Your Student — Link your student(s) to your account using their name and grade level.
  4. 4
    Browse the Menu & Order — Select your desired lunch days and menu items. Orders must be placed by the school's designated weekly deadline.
  5. 5
    Pay Online — Complete your order with a credit or debit card through the secure checkout process.
