Our History

68+ years of Catholic education excellence in Fayetteville

Home / About / Our History

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1956

St. Ann Catholic School has been a beacon of academic and spiritual excellence in downtown Fayetteville for over 68 years, proudly serving the Cape Fear region with distinction.

Founded in 1956, St. Ann Catholic School holds the distinguished honor of being the first all-inclusive school in North Carolina, breaking barriers and welcoming students of any race, color, national origin, and religious preference from its very beginning.

Our Journey Through the Decades

1956

Foundation & Pioneering Spirit

St. Ann Catholic School founded as the first all-inclusive school in North Carolina, embracing students from all backgrounds and breaking down racial barriers in education.

1960s

Military Partnership Begins

Strong partnership with military families established, beginning our tradition of serving those who serve our country. Military dependents become a significant part of our student body.

1970s-80s

Academic Programs Expansion

Curriculum expanded to include comprehensive programs in arts, music, and physical education. Strong academic foundation established with Diocese of Raleigh approval.

1990s-2000s

Technology Integration

Modern educational technology integrated into classrooms. Continued commitment to small class sizes and individualized attention maintains our educational excellence.

2010s

STEM & Enrichment Programs

Introduction of STEM programs, band, and choral music. Expansion of extracurricular offerings while maintaining our core Catholic identity and values.

2020s

Cognia Accreditation & Modern Excellence

Recently completed 5-year Cognia accreditation review. Continued innovation in education while preserving our founding principles of inclusion and excellence.

Our Pioneering Legacy

First All-Inclusive School

Breaking barriers in 1956, we welcomed students regardless of race, color, or religious background—a revolutionary approach that continues today.

Military Family Heritage

68+ years of proud service to military families, with 50% of our current student body being military dependents.

Community Cornerstone

Serving as an academic beacon in downtown Fayetteville, supported by St. Ann Parish and neighboring parishes for nearly seven decades.

Educational Excellence

Generations of students who "entered to learn and departed to serve," making positive impacts in their communities and beyond.

Continuing Our Tradition

Today, St. Ann Catholic School continues to honor its founding principles while embracing modern educational approaches. We maintain our commitment to serving all families, particularly our military community, while providing rigorous academic preparation rooted in Catholic values.

Our small class sizes (11:1 student-teacher ratio), licensed professional faculty, and comprehensive programs from Pre-K through 8th grade ensure that every child receives the individual attention and quality education that has been our hallmark for over six decades.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our motto: "Enter to Learn; Depart to Serve," preparing each student to make a positive difference in the world while staying true to our pioneering spirit of inclusion and excellence.
