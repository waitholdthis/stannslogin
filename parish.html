A Ministry of St. Ann Parish

Click here to visit the parish

St. Ann Catholic School is the primary ministry of St. Ann Parish, reflecting our deep commitment to Catholic education and faith formation in the Fayetteville community.

Located at 365 N Cool Spring Street in downtown Fayetteville, our school and parish share the same address and mission: to serve God and our community through faith, education, and service.

Community of Support

Our school is blessed with the generous support of St. Ann parishioners and neighboring parishes throughout the region. This faith community provides the foundation that allows us to maintain our commitment to Catholic education excellence.

The strong partnership between our parish and school creates a unified community where families, faculty, and parishioners work together to support the spiritual and academic growth of every child.

Faith Woven Throughout School Life

Daily Prayer

Each school day begins and ends with prayer, led by student leaders who guide our school community in reflection and gratitude.

Weekly Mass

Students attend Mass together as a school family, with many serving as altar servers, lectors, gift bearers, and choir members.

Sacramental Life

Students participate in Reconciliation during Advent and Lent, deepening their understanding of God's mercy and forgiveness.

Service Projects

Age-appropriate community service learning helps students live out our motto "Depart to Serve" in practical ways.

School-Parish Partnership

Spiritual Foundation

The parish provides the spiritual foundation and Catholic identity that shapes every aspect of our educational mission.

Financial Support

Parish support helps make Catholic education more accessible to families through subsidies and assistance programs.

Volunteer Network

Parishioners contribute their time and talents to school programs, events, and educational support activities.

Faith Formation

Collaborative approach to faith formation ensures continuity between home, school, and parish worship.

Diocese of Raleigh

As a Catholic school in the Diocese of Raleigh, we operate under the guidance and support of diocesan leadership. Our curriculum and Catholic identity are approved by the Diocese, ensuring that we maintain the highest standards of Catholic education.

This diocesan connection provides us with resources, professional development opportunities, and a network of Catholic schools throughout North Carolina, enriching our educational community and ensuring our continued growth in faith and knowledge.

A Beacon in Downtown Fayetteville

For over 68 years, St. Ann Catholic School has served as an academic beacon in downtown Fayetteville. Our location at the heart of the city reflects our commitment to serving the broader community while maintaining our strong parish roots.

We welcome families from all backgrounds, continuing our tradition as the first all-inclusive school in North Carolina, while providing the spiritual foundation that comes from our deep parish connection.

This unique position allows us to serve both our parish families and the wider Fayetteville community, including our strong partnership with military families who have been part of our community for generations.
