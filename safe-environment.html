Diocese of Raleigh — Office of Child & Youth Protection

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Promise to Protect. Pledge to Heal.

St. Ann Catholic School is committed to providing a safe environment for every child in our community. We follow the Diocese of Raleigh's Safe Environment Program, which requires all clergy, employees, and volunteers working with children to complete background screening and certified safe environment training.

To Report Suspected Abuse

If you suspect a child is being abused or neglected, contact NC Child Protective Services at (919) 527-6380, or call the 24-hour hotline at 1-800-422-4453. To report concerns about clergy or church personnel, visit the Diocese of Raleigh Child & Youth Protection page.

Safe Environment Training

The Diocese of Raleigh Office of Child and Youth Protection hosts regular trainings on recognizing and reporting child abuse and neglect. These sessions are mandatory for all clergy, employees, and those working with children in diocesan programs, including school volunteers.
