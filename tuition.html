Payment Plans

Annual

Pay the full year's tuition in one payment and simplify your year.

Due June 1

Bi-Annual

Split into two equal payments — one in the fall, one in the spring.

June 1 & December 1

Monthly

10 monthly payments through FACTS Tuition Management.

$50 FACTS enrollment fee

Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Discover) are accepted. Monthly payments are processed through FACTS Tuition Management. A $50 application fee applies for the monthly plan.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

FACTS Grant & Aid Assessment

Apply for need-based financial assistance through FACTS at factsmgt.com. A $45 application fee applies. Priority is given to active parish members.

Deadline: March 1

AAMEN & God's Work Our Challenge Awards

Scholarship awards offered through the Diocese of Raleigh for qualifying students and families.

Deadline: March 15, 2026

Scholarship Opportunities

State-funded voucher program available to qualifying North Carolina families. Families must be prepared to pay the registration fee at the time of enrollment. Contact the school office for guidance on applying.

Call (910) 483-3216

Policies

  • Volunteer Requirement: Each family is required to contribute at least 10 volunteer hours per year. Families who do not fulfill this requirement are charged a $100 fee.
  • Early Withdrawal: One month's tuition is charged upon early withdrawal, unless the withdrawal is due to employment-related relocation.
  • Mid-Year Admissions: Tuition is pro-rated for students admitted after the start of the school year.
  • Accepted Payment Methods: Visa, MasterCard, and Discover credit/debit cards are accepted in addition to check and cash.
  • Non-Refundable: All fees and tuition payments are non-refundable once submitted.
